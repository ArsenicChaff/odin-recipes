Step by step:

If you're confused, it's okay. Someone put the ingredients under the instructions, the weirdos. Scroll down, you'll see em!

  1. Prep your food stuffs:

    2. Slice those peppers and onions thin, and heat your olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and saute for 3-5 minutes, and then add the peppers and saute for 5 more minutes. Set aside in a bowl.

  2. Time for the shrooms:

    3. Wipe the skillet clean with a paper towel, then add 1 1/2 Tbsp of butter and melt over medium low heat. Add the sliced muchrooms and saute for 5-8 minutes. They should be golden brown, and then add them to the peppers and onions bowl.

  3. For the fun part, cooking:

    4. Wipe the skillet clean again, and use 1/2 Tbsp of butter for each quesadilla as we go forward. Layer the folowing into the pan, and then repeat for every subsequent quesadilla:

    • 1 tortilla
    • 2 Tbsp cheese
    • 1/3 cup pepper/onion/mushroom stuff
    • 2 Tbsp cheese
    • 1 tortilla

    The bottom tortilla should be golden brown by the time you're finished assembling, and now it's time to flip. Press the quesadilla down to squish it together, then flip and cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Repeat until you're out of food!

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 16 ounces fresh white mushrooms
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded gouda cheese
  • 10 small flour tortillas
  • sour cream
  • fresh cilantro
