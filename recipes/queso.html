Let's learn how to put it all together.

Step by Step:

The full ingredient list can be found below!

  1. First Steps:

    2. Start by melting your butter over medium heat, and saute your onions and then garlic. Add the chilis and tomatoes, all finely diced. Then sprinkle in your cornstarch and stir until combined, and add all your seasonings!

  2. Now Watch it Become Queso:

    3. Slowly drizzle in your evaporated milk, while stirring constantly, and allow the milk to heat up. Keep cooking and stirring until everything has thickened up just a little bit. Turn the heat to low, and stir in your cheese and melt it down!

  3. You're already done, lists just look better with more than 2 points:

    4. Serve that stuff! Eat it with tortilla chips, or pretzel bites.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup finely diced white onion
  • 1 Tablespoon minced garlic
  • 4 oz diced green chiles
  • 1/4 cup diced roma tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon table salt
  • 12 full fat evaporated milk
  • 8 oz grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • corn chips
