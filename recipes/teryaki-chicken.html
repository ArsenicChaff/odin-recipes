Step by Step:

All of the ingredients and quantities are listed below.

  1. Starting with the marinade:

    2. Stir together the soy sauce, brown sugar, water, garlic, ginger, and 1 tablespoon of the cooking oil in a bowl. Place the chicken in another bowl, and pour the marinade over the top. Turn the chicken a couple of times to make sure it's well coated. Marinate for 30 minutes up to one day (refridgerated).

  2. And now we cook:

    3. Heat the other tablespoon of cooking oil in a large skilet over medium heat. Once hot, add the chicken and discard the remaining marinade. Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through (165 degrees F internal). The liquid in the skillet should have reduced to a light glaze covering the chicken.

  3. I mean, that's basically it:

    4. Transfer the chicken to a cutting board and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving. Super simple really!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1.75 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
