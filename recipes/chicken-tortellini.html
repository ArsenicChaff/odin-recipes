Step by Step:

All the ingredients are down below, just incase you need em :)

I also recommend reading all the directions before starting. Helps out I promise!

  1. Whats first:

    2. Grab all your ingredients, and bring a large saucepan with salted water to a boil over high heat. At the same time, heat a large skillet over medium, and add chopped bacon. Stir occasionally for about 8 minutes. Move the bacon to a paper towel lined plate, and make sure to leave about 3 tablespoon of the drippings in the skillet.

  2. Now for the Chicken:

    3. While the bacon cooks, sprinkle the pepper and 1/4 tsp of salt over the chicken. Add the chicken to the skillet once the bacon is finished, leave the drippings in there, and cook over medium high until chicken is browned and cooked through (thickest part of the chicken reaches 165 degreed F). It's okay to reduce the heat if the chicken starts to char before fully cooked. Trasfer to a plate, and chop into small pieces after 10 minutes of resting.

  3. Sundried Tomatoes are Wildly Underrated:

    4. Reduce the heat to medium, and add sundried tomatoes and shallot to the skillet. Cook for about 2 minutes until softened, and add garlic, italian seasoning, crushed red pepper, and the last of the salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant. About 30 to 60 seconds. Add tomato paste, and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.

  4. Put it All Together: (well, half of it)

    5. Pour in your chicken broth (in the skillet), stirring and scraping the bottom of the pan to get all of that flavor. Stir in cream and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Keep it simmering, stirring occasionally, for about 3 to 5 minutes until it thickens just a bit.

  5. Dont Forget about the Tortellini:

    6. Add that tortellini to the boiling water and cook until they float, about 3 to 5 minutes. Drain the tortellini, but save 1 cup of that noodle water. It has flavor. Stir the parm, bacon, chicken, and tortellini into the skillet with the creamy sauce. Cook and stir constantly, until nice and evenly coated. Slowly add that noodle water, until you get a desired consistency. Serve, and garnish with more parm and some basil!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices thick cut bacon
  • 2 oz boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 1/4 teaspon table salt
  • 3/4 cup drained sundried tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped shallot
  • 4 large finely chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 teaspons dried italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspon crushed red pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 20 oz refridgerated cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil leaves
